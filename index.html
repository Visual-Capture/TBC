De Brabant Makelaar B.V.

De Brabant Makelaar B.V. is a real estate agency, who, through an innovative and radical self-developed concept, is able to sell residential properties completely free of charge and at the highest selling price, while still completely unburden the customer.

www.debrabantmakelaar.nl

Aryo Bastiaanssen

Founder

Aryo Bastiaanssen founded TBC HOLDING B.V. at the age of 20, which resulted from a passion for entrepreneurship and real estate.

Through hard work, but by believing more, he managed to buy his first trading investment property within the first six months, without much resources.
At the age of 21 he quit his full-time job to co-found De Brabant Makelaar, where he is now active full-time. That same year, he and his business partner managed to grow their business to the point that they now sell 10+ residential properties a month. They also bought their first rental investment property the same year.

The next step is to franchise the successful concept of De Brabant Makelaar throughout the Netherlands.
They also continue to expand their own investment portfolio with rental and commercial properties, transformations and development projects, both nationally and internationally.
